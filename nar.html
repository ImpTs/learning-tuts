The “Career Tips & Tricks from AE YPN” series originated with the AE YPN Advisory Board seeks to provide resources for new and young REALTOR® association staff, chief staff, and staff specialists alike. 

Each installment is led by a REALTOR® association chief staff or staff specialist, sharing a favorite NAR resource or experience from his or her role at the association. Upcoming installments will be announced in the AE INS and on the AE YPN Facebook page.
